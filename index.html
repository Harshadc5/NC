Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Nikhil Chaudhari different?

Our dentists combine modern technology with personalized care. From same-day crowns and laser fillings to advanced dental implants, we make every visit efficient, comfortable, and tailored to your needs.

Do you offer dental implants?

Yes! Our dentists are leading implant specialists, providing both single-tooth and full-arch dental implants using robot-guided technology for precision and comfort.

Can I straighten my teeth without braces?

Absolutely. We help patients achieve beautifully aligned smiles with clear, comfortable aligners. Schedule a consultation to see if Invisalign treatment is right for you.

What if I don't have dental insurance?

No problem! Our dental savings plan makes it easy to receive high-quality care without the stress of deductibles or waiting periods. Enjoy discounts on routine cleanings, fillings, and even cosmetic procedures.

Can I get a crown or filling in one visit?

Yes. With advanced CEREC and laser technology, we offer same-day crowns and no-shot fillings. That means fewer appointments and a more comfortable experience.

Do you treat TMJ pain and jaw discomfort?

Yes. Our TMJ treatment options include oral appliances, bite adjustments, and therapeutic treatments to reduce muscle tension and restore comfort.
